About Us

The Cyber Club at Krea is a community for coding enthusiasts and computational science explorers. Our vision is to make coding, data science, and AI accessible and engaging through fun activities, competitions, and hands-on projects. We aim to digitize campus processes like parcel management and registration while fostering a collaborative learning environment.

Our mission includes:

  • Offering tutoring for computational courses like ICT and Statistics Programming.
  • Conducting workshops on coding, game development, IoT, and Python.
  • Organizing regular problem-solving meetups and discussions.
  • Engaging in coding competitions and real-world projects using platforms like GitHub.
